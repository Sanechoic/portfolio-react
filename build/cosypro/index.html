Perspective

The conversation around masks has been mostly focused on “mask or no mask” rather than “which mask”. Masks which are difficult or uncomfortable won’t be worn properly or frequently, regardless of how effective they might be.

Insight

Comfort and convenience are as important as filtration and fit when it comes to masks. A simple, low-cost, effective design could affect a positive behavioural change and attitude shift regarding masks.

Vision

A comfortable, convenient, novel mask design which can be made with different material and layer combinations for different applications.

Expanding Markets


To reach a wider audience and focus on our core design competencies in research and future developments, the mask design is available to licensed for manufacture and distribution around the world.
